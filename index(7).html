Why this cast

I chose Max Richter for the music because he mixes cool electronic beats with classic instruments to make the movie sound super emotional. I picked Steven Spielberg to direct because his camera angles are amazing and really make you immersed into the story. Anna B. Sheppard is the costume designer because she knows how to make old-school outfits look exactly like real history. Mckenna Grace and Ryan Reynolds are perfect for Liesel and Hans because they can show that bond between a nice foster dad and his daughter which they seem great for. Imelda Staunton will do great as Rosa because she is great at playing a loud, yelling character who secretly has a heart. Eddie Murphy and Walker Scobell fit Death and Rudy because they bring the perfect mix of comedy to the film that is not entirely funny. Finally, Timothée Chalamet is Max because he is great at playing lonely, hurt characters, and Lil G is the perfect stuntman to handle all the dangerous action and explosion scenes.

Collage explanation

I chose the stack of books to represent Liesel because Liesel always wanted to learn how to read and feel like she was in another world. I picked the accordion because of the feeling that she gets around Hann and she feels protected by him whenever he is around. The soccer ball represents Liesel's lost childhood and life-giving hope. The writing represents Liesel’s dream of using her words to keep her memories and family alive for many generations. The picture of an old small German town street looked like it was in the period that Liesel lived in. This picture brings the actual setting of the book to life as what it would have been like and what you would have seen everyday.
